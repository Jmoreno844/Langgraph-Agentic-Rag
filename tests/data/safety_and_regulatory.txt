ESD Precautions
- Work on a non-conductive surface; use an anti-static wrist strap if available.
- Ground yourself before handling PCBs; handle GPUs, RAM, and motherboards by the edges.

Power Safety
- Disconnect power and hold the case power button for 5â€“10 seconds to discharge before servicing.
- Verify PSU switch is off (O) when connecting 24-pin, 8-pin CPU, and PCIe GPU cables.

Thermal & Mechanical
- Apply a pea-sized amount of thermal paste; do not overtighten coolers.
- Watch for sharp case edges; route cables to avoid fan blades.

Data & Disposal
- Wipe storage devices before RMA/return.
- Recycle e-waste per local regulations; do not dispose of Li-ion batteries in household trash.
