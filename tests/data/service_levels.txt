Severity Levels
- Sev1 (Critical order/payment issue): Response 1h, Update 2h, Target resolution 8h
- Sev2 (Shipping or RMA delay): Response 4h, Update 8h, Target resolution 2 business days
- Sev3 (How-to/Compatibility): Response 1 business day, Resolution best effort

Maintenance Windows
- Scheduled maintenance: Sundays 02:00–04:00 UTC (as announced)
