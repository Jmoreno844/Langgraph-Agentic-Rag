Data Protection
- Encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256) for account and order data.
- Payment processing handled by PCI-DSS compliant providers; we do not store full card details.

Access & Compliance
- Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) for staff with audit logging.
- Minimal data retention aligned with legal and tax requirements.

Incident Reporting
- Report suspected security or privacy incidents via the Support Portal (mark "Security").
- Include order numbers, account email, time range, and relevant screenshots.
