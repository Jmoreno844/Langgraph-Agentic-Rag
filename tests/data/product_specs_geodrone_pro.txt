Flight Time: Up to 55 minutes (standard payload)

Ingress Protection: IP54 (dust and splash resistant)
Operating Temperature: -10°C to 45°C (14°F to 113°F)
Sensors: 48 MP RGB, 24 MP multispectral; optional LiDAR, Thermal
Storage: UHS‑I U3 V30 SD (tested max 512 GB)
